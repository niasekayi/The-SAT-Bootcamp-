What Happens Next

After you apply

  • Review Every application is reviewed individually. We look at your grade level, math background, current score, and goals to find the right group for you.
  • Placement If a spot is available that fits your level, you will receive an email with confirmation and next steps including your squad placement and payment information.
  • Agreement Students and parents will sign the program agreement electronically before payment is processed.
  • Onboarding Once payment is confirmed, you get access to Google Classroom and the program communication hub. You are officially in.

Have questions before applying? Feel free to reach out at thesatbootcamp@gmail.com or register for an Info Session above to learn more first.
