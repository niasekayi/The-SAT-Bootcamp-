Schedule & Pricing

The Sessions

Three sessions across the summer.

Sessions 1 and 2 are 4-week foundational deep-dives. Session 3 is a 2-week intensive focused entirely on execution. You can join one session, two, or all three.

Session 01

Foundation

June 1 – July 1, 2026

4 Weeks

A comprehensive deep-dive into SAT logic and strategy. Three main cohort lectures per week and two Squad Huddles build both knowledge and problem-solving confidence. The final week transitions into a full simulation format.

Session 02

Acceleration

July 13 – August 7, 2026

4 Weeks

A second 4-week session with the same structure as Session 1. Students returning from Session 1 are re-evaluated and placed in a group that reflects their growth. New students are welcome to start here as well.

Session 03

Final Grind

August 10 – August 21, 2026

2 Weeks

A focused 2-week sprint built entirely around test execution. Full timed practice tests, error analysis, and final strategy work. Recommended for all students, especially those taking the SAT soon after the program ends.

Tuition

Simple, transparent pricing.

Each session is priced to make quality prep genuinely accessible. Sessions can be purchased individually or in any combination.

$300

Per Session

Tuition is paid in full before the session start date. This confirms your placement and keeps your spot secured. No partial payments. For students enrolling in more than one session, each payment is due by the date listed in the schedule above.
