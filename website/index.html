About Me

Nia Greene

My name is Nia Greene and I am a Computer Science and Mathematics student at Howard University. Over the past few years I have tutored middle and high school students in math and SAT prep, and one thing I kept noticing is that most programs are not teaching the Digital SAT the way students actually experience it. I built SAT Bootcamp to change that. This is a personalized, strategy-focused program where every student is known by name, placed intentionally, and supported throughout the summer.
